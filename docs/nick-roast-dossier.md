- **Sean is strictly off-limits.** No death jokes, no references to his passing, no "three brothers became..." framing that highlights the loss. Keep it to four living competitors.
- **Keep Rachel jokes respectful** — she's an ally in the roast, not a target. The DVM outranking PA-C is fair game. Anything personal about her is not.
- **The kids (Elanor, Brysen) are referenced only in the context of them roasting Nick.** No jokes targeting the children themselves.
- **Bruce jokes should be affectionate** — he's the patriarch. The humor is that a 70-year-old retired MD might beat his 39-year-old son, not that Bruce is old or diminished.
- **All brothers are from Prineville** — don't use Prineville as a Nick-specific burn since it applies to everyone.
- **Adjust as competition progresses** — add performance-specific jokes as weekly results come in. If Nick has a particularly bad week, deploy targeted material immediately.
